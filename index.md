The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.

## Data Storage
All game progress is stored locally on your device only.

## Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate any third-party services.

The App is safe for users of all ages. No data is collected from any user, including children under 13.
